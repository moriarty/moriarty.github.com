The KUKA youBot

The youBot is based on an omni-directional platform with for swedish wheels, a 5 DoF manipulator and a two finger gripper. The platform is manufactured by KUKA and is commercially available at the youBot-store. It has been designed to work in industrial like environments and to perform various tasks as e.g. fetch & carry or assembly tasks.


The standard configuration of the youBot has been extended by the following additional hardware devices:


  1. *Hokuyo laser range finder:

One laser range finder is mounted in the front and one in the back of the robot to support robust localization, navigation and obstacle avoidance, especially for omni-directional movements.


  1. *ASUS Xtion RGB-D camera:

The RGB-D camera is mounted on the last joint of the manipulator and is used for 3D scene segmentation and object recognition.


* High performance laptop

In addition to the onBoard PC of the youBot, a second laptop with high computational resources is mounted underneath the back platform. It performs the computational expensive perception and planning tasks.
