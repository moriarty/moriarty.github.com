Some of my Experience

Am I an expert? No.

So what am I going to put on this page? likely a website friendly general outline of my resume. I'll even include a link to the real document.

I have experience in web development on the backend side and server administration, working with php, perl and ruby with databases and directory access. I stopped doing websites for free several years ago. However, I do still host a few sites for friends and local groups on my dual xeon server. I occasionally my 8-core server for research projects anaylizing data, video processing or crunching numbers while I sleep, but most of the time it's a on overkill Network Area Storage so I don't mind putting a few small sites as long as they don't ask me anyquestions.

I can't stand technical support. But I have a lot of experience doing it. I've been working at a tech help desk for either hardware or software since grade 11. It's helped me pay for school, my travels and my toys (cameras, bikes and computers) so I can't complain. I do like helping people, and I appreciate when they appreciate the support. But there are some people who take out there anger and frustration on the people trying to help them. Some times there are people who really shouldn't be near a computers without a little bit of training. Lots of people use "The Email" and suddenly think they're experts. I have given my boss my final notice and will not be focusing on my masters research.

And that ends that rant. Hopefuly there is something better to put on this page in the future
