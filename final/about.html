The juicy details:

My name is Alex Moriarty. I am a Computer Science student living in my hometown of Halifax Nova Scotia. I started studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science in the fall of 2008, and will be graduating in the upcomming spring term with the Bachelor of Computer Science. I am still interested in Electrical and Computer Engineering but events unfolded which made continuing the concurrent degree program in a reasonable amount of time impossible.

I do spend most of my time on campus, but I have interests outside of the lab. I'm an avid traveller. I enjoy the outdoors and camping. I've volunteered abroad and taught English in Peru. I was once an extremely active curler and cyclist, but injury and education got in the way.

Although I am accused of being an addict of a certain computer and electronics manufacturer, I deny all these allegations... I do however admit to being a Canon fanboy. I love photography, and remember making my first pin hole camera in elementary school. After spending a while in South East Asia, and coming back to learn my little point and shoot camera's sensor was rubish and nearly every image was too. I invested in my first proper Canon: injury had just taken away a lot of my usual physical activities, and I suddenly didn't want to save up for a motorcycle anymore. So to put a positive spin on a low point in my life, I came out with a great new hobbie.
